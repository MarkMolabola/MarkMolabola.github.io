SplitnStash — AI Receipt Splitter

SplitnStash is an Android mobile app that takes the friction out of splitting restaurant bills. Snap or upload a photo of any receipt and the app sends it to the Gemini AI API, which parses every line item, quantity, subtotal, tax, and tip automatically. From there you add the people in your group, tap the items each person ordered, and hit Calculate — the app instantly produces a per-person breakdown with proportionally distributed tax and gratuity. Every split is saved locally so you can review receipt history and track spending over time through the built-in Report tab.

MDRNJ Fitness App

MDRNJ Fitness is a branded mobile fitness application built for a modern gym experience. The app delivers a full workout management platform — users can browse a curated exercise library, build and schedule custom workout plans, and log completed sessions to track progress over time. A clean, energetic UI ties the experience to the MDRNJ brand identity, while a local SQLite database persists all user data and workout history. The app was built cross-platform with .NET MAUI, enabling deployment to both Android and iOS from a single C# codebase.
